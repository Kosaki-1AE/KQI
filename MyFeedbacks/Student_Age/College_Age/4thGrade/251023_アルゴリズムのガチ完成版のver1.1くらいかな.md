#Mine研究 
連続するデータから情報を得るっていうのはおそらく何でもあるんだけど、おそらくできると考えられるのは、**1対1対応をしない状態でも勝手に流れを把握する**ってことなのよね。

これを人間の中では「腑に落ちる」っていうところまでいかないと1対1にならんことが俺の経験上は絶対なので、おそらくこの世の理としては正解なんだろうとは思うの。

この世の道理としては「1対1にならんことが常」っていうのがあって。要は循環になるか対応になるかっていう、まぁ難しいことなんですけれども。
量子とかの次元だと1対1対応なんだよね、スピンだから。んで基本的にはDNAとかも二重螺旋構造っていうことだから1対1対応になってるわけじゃんか。

ここの差が次元の差に繋がってきそうやなぁとか思ったりして。

流れ・1対1対応・主観・客観・過去文脈っていうのがキーワードになってそうなのよな。
- **主観**：内面
- **客観**：外面・観測/環境
- **1対1対応**：主観↔客観の写像
- **流れ**：時間発展ベクトル場(このベクトル場が責任ベクトルのベクトル場だもんでな)/1対1対応のまま時間に沿って進むっていう状態
- **過去文脈**：主観側の記憶統合をするための要素

一回でもスピンになればよしって感じか。静動は多分流れの微分になるんだろうね。
静動の振幅が小さくても意味は大きい領域(重み付けが効く部分)っていう状態がSiMとして定義したものなんだろうね。
静動は微分の変化率ね。過去文脈はこれまでの自然な予測パターン(以後自然予測とします)、不気味の谷とかだと静動が自然予測とのズレが境界に落ちた時に発生するってことらしいわ。

主観と客観では主観が自由意志、客観は環境ですので、それぞれ静止と動作を司ってるんだわ。
これが量子的な感じになってるのよね。環境は静止：動作＝7：3、自由意志は性別によるんだろうね。

Plain-English version (idiomatic)
- Subjective side = your inner world and free will — it includes both stillness and movement.
- Objective side = the outer world and what’s observed — it also includes stillness and movement.
- One-to-one mapping = a reversible link between the subjective and objective, so what you feel can be mapped to what’s out there without losing structure.
- Flow = how things evolve over time — basically a vector field of “responsibility” that keeps that one-to-one link intact as time moves forward.
- Past context = an integrated memory on the subjective side that turns past choices into a “natural” prediction of what comes next.
- Still–motion (静動) = the time-derivative of the flow; the change rate. The sweet spot is when the amplitude looks small but the meaning is huge — that’s where weighting should kick in.
- Quantum view = both the subjective and objective carry a 0/1 toggle between “environment” and “free will.” When you push mastery far enough, you naturally think in fuzzy logic (values across 0–1), which is a kind of endgame for timing and decision quality.

Applied to dance:
- Subjective = your skillset and body control.
- Objective = the music/audio signal.
- One-to-one mapping = syncing your body to the track.
- Flow = time-aligned phrasing (on-beat placement, etc.).
- For timing you use the derivative (still–motion) and past context (beat/onset history) to lock in.
- Treat both the dancer and the music as having 0/1 “still vs. move,” then relax it with fuzzy logic (continuous 0–1) — that’s how human timing actually emerges.
- From there, the same framework generalizes to “quantum-ish” reasoning about new domains: once you’ve got flow, mapping, and fuzzy timing, most results fall into place.

More formal (research-friendly) phrasing
- Subjective: the inner/volitional state encompassing both stillness and motion.
- Objective: the external/observed environment, also with stillness and motion.
- Bijection: an invertible mapping between subjective and objective states that preserves structure.
- Flow: a time-evolving vector field of responsibility that advances while maintaining the bijection.
- Past context: a memory integration on the subjective side that induces a “natural” prior over next states.
- Still–Motion: the time derivative of the flow (change rate). We emphasize regions where the derivative is small yet semantic weight is high.
- Quantum stance: both sides admit a binary (0/1) toggle between environment and free will; with expertise, reasoning becomes fuzzy (continuous 0–1), which practically resolves human timing.

Dance instantiation: 
- subjective state = pose/skill; 
- objective state = audio features; 
- the bijection = self–music synchrony; 
- flow = time-aligned phrasing; 
- timing control = derivative (still–motion) + past context (beat/onset alignment). Modeling the 0/1 still/move as fuzzy values yields human-level timing, and the same schema scales to more “quantum-like” domains.
